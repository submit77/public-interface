## Why interface

A site becomes an interface when it states where canonical information lives,
offers useful representations of that information, declares its current
capabilities, and makes its limits inspectable.

Public means discoverable. It does not mean that every visitor or agent has
authority to act.

## Current contract

This reference implements representation, publication, evidence, discovery, and
contact routing. Authentication, delegated authority, execution, completion, and
transaction verification are not implemented.
